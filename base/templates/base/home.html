About Me

I graduated from Eckerd College (St. Pete, Fla. BA Theater) in 1978 and soon after toured Europe with ‘Pigeon Drop Comedy Company’, a group formed out of Amsterdam’s infamous ‘Festival of Fools’. Since then I made a name for myself in Amsterdam, Holland where I performed my one man show, ‘Mr. Monday Night’ at the LIDO and worked with various improvisational groups around the world. I can be seen frequently in various Dutch television shows, commercials and international feature films.
